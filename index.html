AI Fitness & Nutrition tracking App

5/30/2025 • Personal Project

My passion for fitness has been a driving force in both my personal life and technical work. After years of self-learning about nutrition, weightlifting, and healthy lifestyle habits, I became frustrated with the limitations of popular fitness apps. Many had confusing user interfaces, lacked accurate nutritional information for beginners, or made tracking food and workouts unnecessarily complicated.

To address these gaps, I designed and built a fitness app that combined my knowledge of health and nutrition with my skills in data engineering and AI. On the backend, I created a structured SQL database to log calories, macronutrients, and workouts, ensuring users could track progress with clarity. To make food tracking seamless, I integrated a computer vision model that allowed users to snap a picture of their meal and instantly receive estimated nutritional breakdowns.

This project not only let me apply Python, SQL, and PyTorch in a real-world product, but also reflected my belief that technology should empower people—especially newcomers—to build healthier habits without confusion. By combining my personal dedication to fitness with technical expertise, I built a tool that is as much about usability and accessibility as it is about advanced AI.

VEX Robotics 5327D Competition Robot

4/12/2022 • Gael Force Robotics Club

This is the robot my competition team and I built and designed to compete in the 2022 VEX Robotics competition. We utilized a tank drive over an x drive due to its consistency and to optimize turning. We also decided to use a more defensive strategy for gameplay, so the tank drive proved to be more stable. However, the most noticeable quality are the air tanks. We used air tanks and pneumatic tubes to securely lift mogos and balls. This method allowed us to consistently score points.

I learned many skills by being on team 5327D, such as reliability, innovative thinking, and leadership. I enjoyed my experience on this team and we all bonded as a team, allowing for us to go through wins and losses with a strong morale.
